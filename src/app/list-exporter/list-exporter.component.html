Recommendations for long or high/medium priority tasks:
  1. Attend TA sessions or Professor Office hours - both are likely on Canvas, but you can always email your professor or check your syllabus.
  2. Make an appointment with the Student Success Center to help with time mangement, organization, study skills, and more. (https://ncf.mywconline.com/)
  3. Make an appointment at the Writing Resource Center for any writing-specific tasks. (https://ncf.mywconline.com/)
  4. Break down your tasks! Manageable pieces are easier to work on. The student success center can help with that.
  5. Finally, if you're just too stuck and don't know what to do, just reach out to your professor. Everyone wants to see you succeed and asking for help is encouraged!
